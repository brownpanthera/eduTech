EduVue

Simplifying access to learning.

EduVue is a Saas-based institute ERP software that focuses on providing affordable end to end learning management system to the school,colleges,coaching centers, tution centers, and other educational institutions.
Developed by Parakh Online Pvt. Ltd. EduVue is an all-in-one solution to streamline the operation of schools,colleges,universities,coaching centers,tution centers etc.EduVue enables the institutions to manage it's Enquiries, Marketing ,Student admission,Virtual Classes ,Online tests with remote proctoring,manage curriculum,report cards,and many more to improve efficiency and productivity in the institutions.We don't just provide a seamless management experience,but also offers exclusive support to the institutions at no extra cost.
